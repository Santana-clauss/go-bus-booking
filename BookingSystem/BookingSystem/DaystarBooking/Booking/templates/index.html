Services

Daystar transportation provides the following services:
1. Transportation from Nairobi campus to Athiriver campus.
2. Transportation from Athiriver campus to Nairobi campus.
We also pick students from the following terminus: Nyayo Stadium, Bellevue, General Motors, City Cabanas, Mlolongo Crossing Bridge, Shalom, and Devki.
The official times for pick up are as follows:
From Nairobi Campus to Athiriver Campus, weekdays- 6:30 am, 1:00 pm, 4:30pm.
From Athiriver campus to Nairobi campus, weekdays- 5:30 am, 11:00 am, 4:30pm

About

Embark on a seamless and stress-free transportation experience with Daystar bus booking System. We've revolutionized the way you travel within the university, erasing delays and confusion from your daily routine. Say goodbye to missed buses and hello to real-time updates, personalized bookings, and a user-friendly interface that puts you in control. Join us in this journey where every bus ride becomes an experience, not just a commute. Welcome aboard!.
